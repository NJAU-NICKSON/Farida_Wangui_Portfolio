Beyond the Books

While my studies and volunteer work are my passion, I also believe in living a well-rounded life. Here are a few things that keep me creative and inspired outside of psychology.

Painting & Art

I find joy in expressing myself through painting. It's my way of capturing emotions and ideas that words can't always describe.

Reading

I love getting lost in a good book, especially fiction and poetry. Reading helps me understand different perspectives and fuels my imagination.

Coffee & Conversation

There's nothing better than a good cup of coffee with a friend. I believe in the power of deep, meaningful conversations to build connections.

Gardening

Tending to my small garden brings me peace and a sense of calm. Watching things grow is a reminder that patience and care lead to beautiful results.

My Approach

My work is rooted in compassion and evidence-based practices. As a student, I'm focused on learning and applying these principles to help others on their journey toward well-being.

1. Understanding

I listen to your story to understand your unique needs and goals without judgment.

2. Collaboration

We work together to develop a personalized plan that feels right for you.

3. Insight

Through our sessions, we'll uncover new insights and perspectives to guide your journey.

4. Growth

The goal is to foster personal growth, allowing you to thrive and celebrate your progress.
